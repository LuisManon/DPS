Hyaluronic Acid

Hyaluronic acid is a non-surgical treatment involving injections of a dermal gel to fill wrinkles, expression lines, or enhance volume and contour in areas such as the lips, cheeks, or jawline. This procedure is ideal for those seeking a quick and effective solution to rejuvenate the face without undergoing surgery. During the initial consultation, your expectations are discussed, and the amount and placement of hyaluronic acid are determined to achieve a natural and harmonious result.

How to Prepare

  • Inform the specialist about any medications you are taking and follow their instructions on which medications to discontinue.
  • Avoid alcohol consumption and aspirin a few days before the treatment to reduce the risk of bruising.
  • Avoid invasive facial treatments or intense exfoliations in the week leading up to the procedure.
  • Hydrate your skin well and follow the specialist’s recommendations to optimize results.

What to Expect from the Treatment

  • Visible improvement in the smoothness of wrinkles and increased volume in the treated areas.
  • A quick procedure, generally between 15 to 30 minutes, with little to no downtime.
  • Possible redness, swelling, or sensitivity at the injection site, which usually subsides within a few days.
  • Immediate results that continue to improve over one or two weeks and can last from 6 to 18 months, depending on the treated area and the type of hyaluronic acid used.
  • A follow-up with the specialist to assess results and plan future sessions if necessary.

Hyaluronic acid treatment is a safe and effective option to improve the appearance of your skin and restore lost volume naturally. By choosing this procedure, you will have professionals dedicated to providing you with the best care and advice to achieve the results you desire. With proper preparation and realistic expectations, you can expect a fresher, rejuvenated, and natural appearance.
