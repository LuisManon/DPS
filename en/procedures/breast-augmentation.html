Breast Augmentation

Breast augmentation, or augmentation mammoplasty, is a surgical procedure that uses implants to increase the size of the breasts or restore volume lost due to weight loss, pregnancy, or aging. This procedure not only enhances physical appearance but can also boost self-esteem and personal confidence. During the initial consultation, your expectations are discussed, and the most suitable type and size of implant are selected based on the patient’s needs and desires.

How to Prepare

  • Have a laboratory test or a medical exam.
  • Take certain medications or adjust your current medications as directed by the surgeon.
  • Get a baseline mammogram before surgery and another afterward to detect any changes in breast tissue.
  • Stop smoking well in advance of the surgery.
  • Avoid taking aspirin, anti-inflammatory drugs, and herbal supplements to reduce the risk of bleeding.

What to Expect from the Procedure

  • An improvement in the shape and size of the breasts according to the discussed expectations.
  • A recovery time that may vary, but generally includes a few days of rest and limitation of physical activities.
  • Possible swelling and temporary discomfort in the first few days after surgery.
  • Visible results almost immediately, although the final appearance will be seen after the swelling has subsided.
  • Regular follow-ups with the surgeon to ensure proper recovery and address any concerns or complications that may arise.

Breast augmentation is a safe and effective procedure that can help you achieve the figure you have always desired, enhancing not only your appearance but also your confidence and overall well-being. By choosing to undergo this procedure, you will be in the hands of dedicated professionals who will guide you every step of the way, ensuring that you feel comfortable and well-informed. With proper preparation and realistic expectations, you can expect results that will make you feel more confident and satisfied with your body. If you are ready to take this important step, we are here to support you and provide the best care possible. Choose to feel good about yourself!
