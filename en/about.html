Dr. Rafael Rivera

Plastic Surgeon in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic

Dr. Rafael Rivera is a plastic surgeon renowned for his vast experience and dedication to his patients. With years of training and practice, he has become a leading figure in the field of aesthetic and reconstructive surgery. His personalized approach and commitment to excellence have earned him the trust and respect of those who have turned to him to improve their quality of life.

Throughout his career, Dr. Rivera has participated in numerous national and international conferences, including in countries like Mexico, Guatemala, and Costa Rica, where he has shared his knowledge and stayed at the forefront of the latest techniques in plastic surgery. Additionally, he has undertaken extensive training in aesthetic surgery, honing his skills to provide natural and satisfying results for his patients.

Dr. Rivera has performed a wide variety of successful interventions, helping his patients achieve their aesthetic goals and improving their self-esteem. His passion for plastic surgery is evident in every procedure he performs, always focusing on the safety and well-being of each person.

Currently, Dr. Rivera serves as a plastic, reconstructive, and aesthetic surgeon at the Center for Plastic Surgery and Liposuction (CECILIP), located at Arroyo Hondo, Santo Domingo. His commitment to excellence and attention to detail make him a reliable choice for those looking to transform their image and improve their quality of life.
